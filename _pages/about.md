<br>
Yong Cheng is currently a Ph.D. student at George Mason University, advised by Prof. [Yotam Gingold](https://cragl.cs.gmu.edu/), beginning in 2025. His research interests focus on computer graphics, particularly geometry processing.

Previously, he worked on high-definition mapping for autonomous-driving systems at [NIO](https://www.nio.com/). Prior to it, he was part of the geometric engine team at [IDEA](https://www.idea.edu.cn/), where he contributed to performance optimization and intersection algorithms for Computer-Aided Design (CAD).

He earned his Master's degree in Computer Science from Washington University in St. Louis in 2023, where he conducted a research project under the supervision of Prof. [Tao Ju](https://www.cse.wustl.edu/~taoju/).
